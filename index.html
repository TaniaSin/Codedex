Brooklyn Stoop Sale

For folks in South Brooklyn

Who is invited?

Anyone who lives in the area and likes pre-loved possessions

Can I bring stuff?

You are more than welcome to.

Recommended timing: 2pm
What to sell?

If you don't love it or haven't worn it for years, sell it. Sell puzzles if you have all the pieces.

What to bring?

You can bring clothes, furniture, games, bags, etc

Place? Time? Date?

3pm-8pm on 22 Aug at Caroll Gardens

You can find the google maps link on next page
Tips for sellers

Arrive early to set up your stuff.Clearly price each item (unless you like to haggle).
